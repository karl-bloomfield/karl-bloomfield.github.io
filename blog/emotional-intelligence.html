Research EQ and IQ.

What is EI? Emotional Intelligence (aka EI or "emotional quotient" EQ) is ones ability to perceive, interpret, demonstrate, control, evaluate and use emotions to communicate with and relate to others effectively and constructively.

How is EQ different form IQ? EQ refers to an ability, where IQ is a number used to express an individuals overall mental ability.

Why is EQ important? EQ is important because it is essential for positive interpersonal communication.
